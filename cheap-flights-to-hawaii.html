Hawaii is America's most beloved vacation destination and arguably its most beautiful. The islands offer stunning beaches, volcanic landscapes, and unique culture — but flights from the mainland can be expensive if you don't know the strategies. With the right approach, you can find genuinely affordable Hawaii airfare.

The four main visitor islands are Oahu (Honolulu), Maui, Kauai, and the Big Island (Hawaii). Oahu has the most flights and typically the lowest fares since it's the biggest hub. Maui is the most popular resort island. Kauai is the most tranquil and remote. The Big Island offers the most diverse experiences. Our agents at (866) 228-4952 help you choose the best island for your interests and budget.

The cheapest months for Hawaii flights are September, October, and early November. Summer (June-August) and winter holidays (December-January) are the most expensive. Spring Break (March-April) also sees price spikes. Saving $200-400 per ticket is possible just by choosing September over July.

Airlines serving Hawaii from the mainland include Hawaiian Airlines, Alaska Airlines, United, Delta, American, and Southwest. Hawaiian Airlines has the most routes between islands.
