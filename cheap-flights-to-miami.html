Miami is one of America's most glamorous destinations — and thankfully, one where finding affordable flights is genuinely possible if you're strategic. Miami International Airport (MIA) is one of the busiest airports in the country with flights from virtually every major US city.

Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL) is 30 miles north of Miami and serves as a budget airline hub, particularly for Spirit and Southwest. FLL flights are often $30-100 cheaper than MIA, and Uber/Lyft to Miami Beach takes about 45 minutes. Our agents at (866) 228-4952 automatically compare both airports for your booking.

Winter (December-April) is Miami's peak tourist season with warm, dry weather — and higher flight prices. If you can visit in late spring or early summer, prices drop significantly. Hurricane season (June-November) brings lower fares, though September and October do carry weather risk.
