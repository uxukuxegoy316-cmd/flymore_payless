Hidden fees have become the airline industry's biggest revenue source, generating billions in additional income beyond ticket prices. US airlines collected over $7 billion in baggage fees alone in recent years, and that's before counting seat selection, booking, and other charges.

Baggage fees are the most common hidden cost. What looks like a $150 ticket can become $220 when you add a checked bag. Different airlines have vastly different baggage policies — Southwest still includes checked bags free, while Spirit charges for even carry-ons. When you call (866) 228-4952, our agents explain exactly what's included in your fare before you pay.

Seat selection fees are another major trap. Airlines now charge $10-75 per seat to choose where you sit, with premium seats costing even more. If you don't pay, you're assigned whatever is left. Our agents can select seats during booking and advise which seats offer the best value.

Booking fees are the most unnecessary cost. Online platforms and travel sites add $5-25 per ticket as a service fee. When you call (866) 228-4952, there are zero booking fees. We charge nothing on top of the airline's published fare. What we quote is exactly what you pay.
